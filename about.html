Welcome to EduHome, your all-in-one solution for managing your university life effortlessly. We understand that university can be a whirlwind of assignments, deadlines, and schedules, and that's why we've created a platform designed to simplify your academic journey.

To enhance your scheduling experience, we seamlessly integrate with Google Calendar. You can sync your University timetable with your Google Calendar, ensuring that all your appointments and commitments are in one place, accessible on your desktop or mobile device.
